TicTacToe game with 2 players.
Player 1 is 'X'
Player 2 is 'O'

Each player takes a turn and chooses where they would like to make their move.

If the desired spot is valid, their character will be placed there and the updated board will print.
If not valid, the programn will prompt the player to choose a different, valid, spot.

The game is over when one player wins, or all spaces are filled and a tie is called.
To win: 3 spots across, down, or diagonal.
